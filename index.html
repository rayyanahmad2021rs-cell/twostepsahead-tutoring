Money-Back Guarantee on Packages

If the student's goals are not achieved, we offer an 80% refund on all tutoring packages. We are confident in our tutors and our methods — your success is our priority.

Terms & Conditions

The 80% refund guarantee is subject to the following conditions:

  • The student must complete every session included in the package in full
  • The student must complete every question and past paper assigned by the tutor
  • The student must score to the required standard on their mock assessments as set by the tutor
  • The student must follow through fully with the tutoring plan and complete all assigned work on time

Refunds are only applicable to package purchases and do not apply to per-session bookings or one-off services.
